Celeritas

The scientific method is an integral part of any kind of formal research, but yet how many of us know how it works? Where do we learn how to make good, sound scientific arguments? Upon realising that there wasn't a pre-existing forum on campus for these kinds of rigorous discussions, we decided to make one ourselves! Celeritas is for anyone and everyone who wishes to better improve themselves in the domains of science, logic, and public speaking. So don't be shy, just turn up for our sessions and have fun :)

NOTE : This is an open forum for anyone who wishes to take part, you don't need to be a member of stellaria to benifit from these sessions. We're here for you, to grow together. We look forward to seeing you here ;)

The topics we discuss ranges all the way from anime to astrophysics, "conspiracy" theories to human psychology. All we ask is that participants refrain from personal attacks, discussions regarding the functioning of the university, and humbly request everyone to strictly refrain from discussing religious beliefs. Theism and atheism are not topics the forum shall deal with, but feel free to discuss them on your own free time.
